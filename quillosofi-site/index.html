What it does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don’t.

Two Views, One Document

Quillscript gives you Notion-style continuous scroll. Flip the toggle and Quillginate renders the same content in paginated page frames. No data duplication, no export step.

Quillscript

A single Tiptap editor instance per canvas. Cross-page selection works natively. Notion-style page header with emoji, cover gradient, and Oldenburg serif title.

Quillginate

Word-style ruler, indent markers, hard page breaks (Mod-Enter), spread mode, outline rail, and a paginator that auto-reflows as you type. Designed for layout review.

Spaces & Canvases

Organize your writing in Spaces — groups of canvases with drag-reorder, double-click rename, emoji picker, and twelve chalkboard cover gradients.

Real Auto-Updates

electron-updater with alpha and beta channels. Download progress, in-session install prompt with countdown, per-version dismissal. No silent close-and-reinstall.

Full Data Export

Export your canvases as DOCX or PDF from Settings. Your writing is yours — no lock-in, no proprietary formats hidden behind a paywall.

The workflow

One document.
Two views.

01

Create a Canvas

Start a new canvas inside any Space. Give it an emoji, pick a cover gradient, and start writing.

Canvases are organized in Spaces — your worldbuilding saga, your short fiction, your research notes. Each canvas keeps its own content, settings, and Quillginate state independently.

📖
Choose a cover
02

Write in Quillscript

The default mode: one editor, continuous scroll, Notion-style page header. Just write.

Cross-page selection works natively because there’s only one editor per canvas. Format with the top toolbar or keyboard shortcuts.

Quillscript

03

Flip to Quillginate

When you want to review layout, hit the toggle. Same document — now in paginated page frames.

The paginator splits your document into pages with a Word-style ruler, spread mode, and an outline rail. Add hard page breaks (Mod-Enter) that the paginator respects. Flip back anytime — zero data loss.
